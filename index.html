11 — Motion

Calm & Subtle

FrontrowMD motion is never flashy or attention-seeking. All durations are under 300ms. Prefer subtle fade-ups on scroll and gentle lifts on hover. Click or hover the boxes below.

Fade Up (0.3s)

Click to replay. Used for section entry on scroll.

Card Hover Lift

translateY(-2px) + elevated shadow. Cards on hover.

Button Lift

translateY(-1px). Gentle lift for buttons only.

Motion Rules

✓ Do

Subtle fade-ups on scroll entry. Slight hover lifts (1–2px). Durations under 300ms. Ease or cubic-bezier easing.

✗ Don't

Spinning, pulsing, or bouncing effects. Dramatic slides or zooms. Attention-seeking animations. Durations over 300ms.

12 — Rules

Do's & Don'ts

Brand guardrails to maintain consistency across every touchpoint.

✓ Do

Use Libre Baskerville for headlines; Inter 400 for body text
Use the light wash gradient for backgrounds — never flat white
Use pill-shaped buttons (border-radius: 50px)
Maintain generous whitespace using the 8px grid
Use Deep Navy (#172C45) for text on light backgrounds
Use Midnight Blue (#020F18) instead of pure black
Keep animations under 300ms, calm and subtle

✗ Don't

Use non-brand colors (warm tones, greens, purples)
Modify, recolor, or distort the logo
Use flat white (#FFFFFF) as a page background
Use sharp-cornered (0px radius) cards
Use pure black (#000000) for text
Use spinning, bouncing, or flashy animations
Use non-brand fonts (only Libre Baskerville + Inter)
