FrontrowMD Brand Style Guide

A comprehensive guide to our visual identity, design system, and brand voice. Professional. Trustworthy. Sophisticated.

01 — INTRODUCTION

Brand Identity Overview

Who We Are

FrontrowMD connects wellness brands with a network of 1,700+ clinicians who share products on patient discount pages to build trust and credibility. We also operate ClinicianAI — a clinician-powered AI shopping assistant embedded on brand product pages.

Brand Personality

Professional • Trustworthy • Calm • Sophisticated • Modern • Credible


Medical heritage meets modern marketing. Elegant serif typography. Muted, monotone blues and teals. Never loud or flashy.

02 — IDENTITY

Logo Guidelines

The FrontrowMD logo consists of our custom caduceus icon paired with the Libre Baskerville wordmark "frontrowMD" (lowercase "frontrow", uppercase "MD"). These elements always appear together unless using the standalone icon.

Do's

  • Use the primary logo on white/light backgrounds
  • Use the reversed logo on dark backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space equal to the "M" height
  • Use provided logo files only

Don'ts

  • Don't stretch or distort the logo proportions
  • Don't add gradients or effects to the logo
  • Don't use non-brand colors on the logo
  • Don't rotate or add shadows to the logo

ClinicianAI Branding

When creating content specifically about ClinicianAI (the clinician-powered AI shopping assistant product), use the Graphic Gems decorative pattern as a branded background element to distinguish it from general FrontrowMD content.

Graphic Gems Usage

The geometric gem/crystal pattern adds visual distinction to ClinicianAI materials:

  • Use as a background texture for ClinicianAI social graphics
  • Apply at reduced opacity behind ClinicianAI feature callouts
  • Include in ClinicianAI product pages and marketing materials
  • Available in blue and white background versions

04 — TYPOGRAPHY

Typography System

Libre Baskerville is our only typeface — an elegant serif that conveys professionalism, trustworthiness, and medical authority. It's used for all text: headlines, body copy, captions, and buttons.

06 — BRAND VOICE

Tone & Messaging

Writing Style

Professional but approachable. Confident and credible — backed by data, clinician networks, and science. Clear and concise — no jargon-heavy marketing speak. Trust-forward — emphasize verification, endorsement, and unbiased credibility.

Key Messages

"Turn doctors into your D2C brand's biggest advocates" • "Endorsed by doctors, trusted by all" • 1,700+ clinician network • Uncompensated clinician endorsements • Science-backed, third-party validated • ClinicianAI: clinician-powered AI shopping assistant

Words to Use

Trust • Verified • Endorsed • Clinician-backed • Science-backed • Credible • Uncompensated • Wellness • Patient-first • Transparent • Evidence-based • Independent • Personalized

Words to Avoid

Cheap • Influencer • Sponsored (the whole point is uncompensated) • Hack • Miracle • Cure • Guaranteed • Discount (in negative context)

DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Design Principles

Calm & Sophisticated

Use generous whitespace, subtle animations (under 300ms), and the muted color palette. Never flashy or attention-seeking.

Consistent System

Stick to the 8px spacing scale, use Libre Baskerville exclusively, and maintain the monotone palette across all touchpoints.

Medical Heritage

The caduceus icon, serif typography, and muted blues create a bridge between medical credibility and modern marketing.
